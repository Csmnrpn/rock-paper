Rock Paper & Robots

The year is 2350. Humanity has been fighting a losing war against Robots for the past hundred years. Their single standing stronghold has been surrounded by the merciless robot armies.

You have been chosen to fight a duel againts the RoboChampion. The fate of all humans rests in your hands.
